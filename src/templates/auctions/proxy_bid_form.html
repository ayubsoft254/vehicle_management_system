What is Proxy Bidding?

Proxy bidding allows you to set a maximum bid amount. The system will automatically bid on your behalf up to your maximum, increasing your bid by the minimum increment only when necessary to maintain your leading position.

How it works:

  1. 1 You set your maximum bid amount
  2. 2 The system bids the minimum needed to keep you in the lead
  3. 3 Your actual bid increases automatically as others bid
  4. 4 You'll be notified if someone outbids your maximum
